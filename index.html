Nearby Destinations

Shankumugham Beach

Shanghumugham beach is considered as the ‘Arattukadavu’ of Sri Ananthapadmanabhan - the presiding deity of the city.

Aazhimala Shiva Temple

Dedicated to Shiva, the temple is known for the 18 m (58 ft) tall Gangadhareshwara sculpture, which is the tallest Shiva sculpture in Kerala

Chitharal Jain Monuments and Bhagavati Temple

Cave containing Rock-cut sculptures of Thirthankaras and attendent deities carved inside and outside dating back to 9th Century A.D

Kuthiramalika Palace

Built by Swathi Thirunal, is famous for its intricate wood carvings.

Padmanabhaswamy Temple

Dedicated to Lord Vishnu, is one of India’s richest temples.

Trivandrum Zoo

Established in 1857, is home to diverse wildlife.

Napier Museum

Showcases Kerala’s rich cultural heritage and art.

Kovalam Beach

Famous for its crescent-shaped shores and lighthouse.

Poovar Island

Serene backwaters with golden sand and palm-fringed beauty.

Padmanabhapuram Palace

Renowned for its wooden architecture and murals.
